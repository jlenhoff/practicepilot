Effective: April 16, 2026 Operator: Houston Heights Consulting LLC Product: Practice Pilot
Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Practice Pilot is a marketing automation platform built for therapy practice owners. It is operated by Houston Heights Consulting LLC, a Texas limited liability company.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We built Practice Pilot to handle sensitive business data responsibly. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

In plain terms: Practice Pilot uses your data only to run the automations you set up. Your practice information, your clients' information, and your social content stay yours.

Section 1

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate the Practice Pilot platform and its automations. This includes:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and practice name provided during registration
  • Practice configuration data — information you enter to configure your automations, including practice details, specialties, tone preferences, and content settings
  • Social media account data — when you connect Facebook or Instagram accounts, we receive OAuth tokens and profile identifiers necessary to publish content on your behalf
  • Referral partner data — names, email addresses, roles, and organizations you enter into the referral partner system
  • Generated content — social posts, Google Business Profile posts, and outreach emails generated by the platform on your behalf
  • Usage and log data — system logs, automation run history, and error logs used to maintain platform reliability
Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to operate and improve Practice Pilot. Specifically:

  • To generate and publish social media content to your connected Facebook Page and Instagram account
  • To generate and publish Google Business Profile posts on your behalf
  • To send referral partner outreach emails from your designated practice email address
  • To log automation activity and provide you with reporting via your connected Google Sheet
  • To send you system notifications and content previews via email
  • To troubleshoot errors and maintain platform reliability

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the services you have configured.

Section 3

Meta Platform Data

Practice Pilot integrates with Meta's platforms (Facebook and Instagram) through the Meta Graph API. When you connect your Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account to Practice Pilot:

  • We request only the permissions required to publish content on your behalf — specifically instagram_content_publish, instagram_basic, pages_manage_posts, and pages_read_engagement
  • Access tokens are stored securely and used only to publish content you have authorized through the platform
  • We do not access your personal Facebook profile, friends list, messages, or any data beyond what is required for content publishing
  • We do not sell, transfer, or share Meta platform data with any third party
  • You may revoke Practice Pilot's access to your Meta accounts at any time through your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites

Meta compliance note: Practice Pilot's use of Meta platform data complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Meta Developer Policies. We do not use Meta data for any purpose prohibited by those policies.

Section 4

Third-Party Services

Practice Pilot integrates with the following third-party services to deliver its functionality:

  • Anthropic Claude API — used to generate social media content, Google Business Profile posts, and referral outreach emails. Content prompts and generated text are processed by Anthropic's API. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.
  • Meta Graph API — used to publish content to your Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account.
  • Google Apps Script and Google Sheets — the platform runs on Google's infrastructure. Your configuration and content logs are stored in a Google Sheet in your own Google account.
  • Gmail / Google Workspace — used to send referral outreach emails and system notifications from your configured practice email address.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics platforms that track your users.

Section 5

Data Storage and Security

Practice Pilot is architected so that your data stays primarily within your own Google Workspace. Your practice configuration, content logs, referral partner lists, and outreach history are stored in a Google Sheet that lives in your Google account — not on our servers.

API keys and access tokens are stored as Google Apps Script Script Properties, which are encrypted at rest by Google and accessible only within your script environment.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data that passes through our systems. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 6

Data Retention

Because your data is stored in your own Google Sheet, you control retention directly. You may delete any row, tab, or the entire spreadsheet at any time.

Access tokens and API keys stored in Script Properties can be deleted through the Apps Script Project Settings at any time.

If you discontinue use of Practice Pilot, we recommend revoking the app's access to your Meta accounts and deleting any stored API keys from your Script Properties.

Section 7

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data Practice Pilot holds about you or your practice
  • Correct inaccurate data in your practice configuration
  • Delete your data by removing it from your Google Sheet and revoking connected app access
  • Revoke Practice Pilot's access to your Meta accounts at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used by contacting us directly

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or California, additional rights may apply under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Please contact us to exercise those rights.

Section 8

Children's Privacy

Practice Pilot is a business-to-business platform intended for use by therapy practice owners and healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Section 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the platform evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Practice Pilot after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
