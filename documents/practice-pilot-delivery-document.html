02 — How to Use It

Daily Operation

Most automations fire without any action from you. For automations that require a human decision first, use the Practice Pilot Trigger Form.

Intake form submitted Fires automatically. No action needed. Check your internal alert email and review the intake tracking sheet.
New client confirmed Submit Trigger Form → "New Client Confirmed." Onboarding sequence fires automatically.
Client no-shows Submit Trigger Form → "No-Show." Select first appointment checkbox if applicable. Follow-up sequence fires automatically.
Client cancels Submit Trigger Form → "Cancellation." Select cancel type. Correct sequence fires automatically.
Client discharges Submit Trigger Form → "Client Discharge." Select discharge type. Note: Practice Initiated track holds for your review before sending.
Slot opens for waitlist Submit Trigger Form → "Slot Available." Enter clinician, day/time, and insurance. Waitlist outreach fires after your review.

04 — Maintenance

Keeping It Running

This system is designed to run without ongoing maintenance. Here's what to watch for:

Monthly
Check your tracking sheets

Scan intake, no-show, and discharge sheets to confirm rows are populating correctly. If a row stops appearing, the form may have been modified.

Quarterly
Review automated email copy

Read through your welcome, onboarding, and follow-up emails. Update practice details, addresses, or clinician names if anything has changed.

Annually
Verify BAA is still active

Confirm your Google Workspace BAA or M365 BAA is current. BAAs don't expire automatically but your subscription must remain active.

As needed
Adding a new clinician

Update the clinician dropdown in your Trigger Form and the clinician list in the Apps Script CONFIG block. No logic changes needed.

As needed
If something stops working

Check that no one has modified column order in the tracking sheet or rebuilt the Trigger Form. Column index changes are the most common cause of automation breaks. Contact joe@privatepraxis.co within your support window.
